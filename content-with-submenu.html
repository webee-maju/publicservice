Forms and Templates

The Selection and Appointment Process

  1. The “Manual on the Selection and Appointment Process under Delegated Authority in the Malta Public Service” (available by clicking here ) is aimed at HR practitioners within Ministries and Departments and at members on Selection Boards. It outlines the whole process, starting with the drafting and issue of a call for applications, the appointment and duties of a Selection Board, the interview and assessment process, and the appointment of successful candidates.

  2. A Presentation on "Conducting an Effective Interview", as a guide to members on Selection Boards, is available for download by clicking here.

Documents pertaining to Selection Boards

The following templates/documents are aimed at persons appointed on Selection Boards:-

  1. Declaration Form regarding Conflicts of Interest (PSC Form No. 6)

  2. Consanguinity/Affinity Chart

  3. Declaration Form regarding Eligibility of Candidates (PSC Form No. 7)

  4. Assessment Sheet ;

  5. Selection Board Report

  6. Result Sheet

Standard Application Form

The application form for recruitment in the Public Service is available for downloading and printing. Download the Application Form in English (English text) or in Maltese (Maltese text).
